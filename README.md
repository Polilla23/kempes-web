# kempes-web

Front-end and Back-end of Kempes Web.

Here you will find everything related to the development of the Kempes Master League website. The Kempes Master League is an online FIFA league that has been running for over 13 years, where teams and players are unique and unrepeatable. It boasts more than 40 managers/teams, each with over 50 players, and features cups, leagues, and even a transfer market.

It is the dream come true for every player of the old Master League on Play 1.

This website will include:

- Admin dashboard
- Transfers
- Creation of leagues/cups
- Fixtures
- Statistics (top scorers, assist providers, scores, yellow cards, red cards, injuries)
- A history section containing everything related to a team and a player (titles, promotions, relegations, transfers, results, etc.)"
